Introduction to Machine Learning with the Tidyverse 

        		
        
                
            
                                 

        		
        

        
            
        
    

    

    



    

        

            
                
                
                                             

            		

            
    

        

            These workshops provide a gentle introduction to supervised machine learning: concepts, methods, and R code. Participants learn how to train and assess predictive models with several common machine learning algorithms, as well as how to do feature engineering to improve the predictive accuracy of their models. We focus on teaching intuitive explanations of the models and best practices for predictive modeling. Along the way, we introduce several core tidymodels packages, which provide a grammar for modeling that makes it easy to the right thing, and harder to accidentally do the wrong thing.
