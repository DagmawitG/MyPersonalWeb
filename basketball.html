Basketball-My Favorite Sport

Last updated on May 3,2020      . 3 min read .     Interests

My favorite leisure activity is playing, watching, reading, really anything to do with basketball. Basketball is by far my favorite sport in the world.The sport of basketball is known as a fun past time for any person young or old. Basketball is a great way of exercise and a great way to have fun with friends and possibly make some new ones.
With this Passion, I am developing my skills with the help of training. However, basketball is much more than fun; it is a great workout that can help you burn calories, build endurance, improve balance and coordination, develops self-discipline and concentration, and much more.
